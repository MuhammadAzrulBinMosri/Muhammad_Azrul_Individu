ABOUT ME

My name is Muhammad Azrul bin Mosri and I'm 20 years old. Most of my friend call me Azrul but my family call me Rul. I was born on 14th June 2000 in Hospital Banting, Kuala Langat, Selangor. My mother gave birth of me and my sibling in the same hospital. I have 4 siblings including me and 3 sisters. As you all might guess, I'm the youngest in the family. I live in Kampung Kanchong Darat, Banting Selangor Darul Ehsan.

My primary school life begun when i was 7 years old. The school is Sekolah Kebangsaan Kanchong Darat. I pass with flying colours in UPSR with 4A 1B. Then, I went to secondary school, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Telok Datok. I managed to do well in PMR in Form 3 and continued my Form 4 in the same school. Once again, I did quite well in SPM and I have been offered to enter Universiti Teknologi Mara Negeri Sembilan, Campus Rembau in 2018. I have been offered to enter information management cost. Now, I am part 5 student and this is my last sem for Diploma.
